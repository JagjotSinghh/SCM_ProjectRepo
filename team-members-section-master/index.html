About team

Meet the innovative trio of computer science enthusiasts: Ishit Sharma, Jajgot Singh, and Jai Anand. These dynamic students joined forces to bring their collective passion for technology to life by creating a cutting-edge social media dashboard website.

Ishit Sharma: With a keen eye for design and a knack for user experience, Ishit Sharma played a pivotal role in shaping the aesthetic appeal of the website. His dedication to creating an intuitive and visually engaging interface is evident in every pixel of the dashboard.

Jajgot Singh: A coding virtuoso, Jajgot Singh brought his programming prowess to the table, ensuring that the website not only looks great but functions seamlessly. His expertise in backend development and data management laid the foundation for a robust and efficient social media dashboard.

Jai Anand: The visionary of the team, Jai Anand, contributed strategic insights that elevated the project to new heights. His ability to conceptualize features that enhance user engagement and provide valuable analytics showcases his forward-thinking approach to social media management.

Together, Ishit, Jajgot, and Jai have crafted a social media dashboard that reflects their combined skills, dedication, and passion for computer science. This project stands as a testament to their collaborative spirit and their commitment to pushing the boundaries of what technology can achieve.
